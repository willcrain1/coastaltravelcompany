Our signature collections are pre-defined project engagements built to deliver a complete, campaign-ready visual library — each designed around a different scope, timeline, and creative intention.

Collection 01 The Editorial Stay

Immersive & Complete

Our most comprehensive offering. A multi-day on-property engagement that captures the full breadth of your hotel — architecture, interiors, landscape, lifestyle, food & beverage, and ambient film. Designed for properties preparing for a major launch, rebrand, or campaign season.

The result is a complete, campaign-ready visual library that positions your property for desire across every platform and touchpoint.

Includes

  • Full editorial photography library (200+ selects)
  • Lifestyle & talent direction
  • Short-form video (3–5 Reels)
  • Ambient property film (60–90 sec)
  • Full retouching & color grading
  • 3–4 day on-property engagement

Collection 02 The Fashioned Weekend

Style-Forward & Curated

A focused two-day shoot centered on lifestyle, fashion-forward human presence, and short-form video. Ideal for properties that already have a strong architectural library and need to inject personality, warmth, and movement into their visual identity.

Think golden-hour poolside moments, morning coffee on a terrace, a couple wandering cobblestone streets at dusk. The feeling of a perfect weekend, captured.

Includes

  • Curated lifestyle photography (80–100 selects)
  • On-camera talent & styling direction
  • Short-form video (2–3 Reels)
  • Detail & amenity capture
  • Full retouching & color grading
  • 2 day on-property engagement

Collection 03 The Signature Story

Brand-Defining & Aspirational

A campaign-level engagement built to define or redefine how the world perceives your property. A deep, narrative-driven project — combining photography, film, and styled editorial sequences into a single, powerful brand story.

Suited for luxury properties with significant creative ambition: flagship campaigns, international media features, or a complete brand repositioning.

Includes

  • Full editorial photography library (300+ selects)
  • Campaign-level talent & wardrobe styling
  • Short-form video series (5+ Reels)
  • Hero brand film (2–3 min)
  • Full post-production & color suite
  • 4–5 day on-property engagement
