The Process

How Walkthroughs Are Made

01
Capture

We record slow, overlapping video passes of each space — suites, lobbies, terraces — using 4K camera with steady, deliberate movement to maximise overlap between frames.

02
Processing

The footage is processed through Gaussian Splatting algorithms that reconstruct a photorealistic 3D scene from the video frames — preserving colour, light, and texture with near-photographic fidelity.

03
Delivery

The finished scene is delivered as an interactive web viewer — embeddable on your property website, shareable via link, and included in the client delivery gallery alongside all photos and video.
