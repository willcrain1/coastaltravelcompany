The Photographer

William Crain

Founder & Creative Director

William Crain is the founder and lead photographer behind Coastal Travel Company. Based in Palm Beach, Florida, he specializes in editorial hotel photography that blends architecture, lifestyle, and high-fashion energy into timeless visual narratives.

His work is rooted in the belief that great hospitality photography should feel aspirational, not artificial — and that the best images are the ones that make a traveler feel something before they even book.

Background

With a background in editorial and fashion photography, William brings a distinctive eye to hospitality — one shaped by years of working with publications, brands, and destinations that demand a higher standard of visual storytelling.

He founded Coastal Travel Company to fill a gap he kept encountering: luxury properties that deserved world-class imagery but were working with photographers who treated hotels like real estate shoots.

Every project William takes on is treated as a creative partnership — not a vendor relationship. He works closely with property teams to understand their brand, their guest, and the feeling they want to evoke.
