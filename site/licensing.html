Standard Commercial Use

Every license includes these uses as standard — no extra approval needed.

  • Hotel or resort official website — all pages, image galleries, booking modules
  • Social media channels (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, TikTok) owned by the property
  • Email marketing and newsletters sent to the property's own subscriber list
  • Online travel agent listings (Booking.com, Expedia, Hotels.com, Google Travel)

  • Presentations to ownership groups, investors, and travel trade partners

Plain English

Key Terms Explained

What is commercial use?

Commercial use means using the images to promote your business — on your website, in advertising, in printed materials, or on social media. All Coastal Travel Company licenses cover commercial use for the contracted property.

Digital vs. print — what's the difference?

Digital use covers any screen: website, social media, digital ads, email. Print covers physical materials like brochures, signage, and menus. Standard licenses include both, with large-format advertising print (billboards, national magazine spreads) requiring a conversation first.

What does duration mean?

Duration is how long you're licensed to use the images from the delivery date. A 3-year license on The Fashioned Weekend means you can use the images commercially for 3 years. After that, the images should be removed from active use unless the license is renewed or extended.

What does geographic scope cover?

All Coastal Travel Company licenses are worldwide by default — there are no territorial restrictions. You can use your images to market your property to guests anywhere in the world, in any country's media.

What is exclusivity and do I need it?

Standard licenses are non-exclusive — Coastal Travel Company retains the right to display delivered images in the portfolio and use them to market our services. Most hotel clients do not require exclusivity. If you're running a sensitive campaign, rebranding a flagship property, or have specific contractual restrictions, exclusivity can be added for an additional fee.

Can I share images with my PR agency?

Yes. Your PR, marketing agency, or any agency working on behalf of your property can use the images under your license for property promotion. They cannot use the images for their own agency marketing without a separate agreement.

Enhanced Rights

Extended License Options

Upgrades are available at any time — before or after delivery. All extended options are priced per project.

01

Extended Duration

Extend your license beyond the standard term — to 10 years or in perpetuity. Ideal for images you plan to use in evergreen campaigns or brand identity materials with no defined end date.

02

Exclusivity

Exclusive rights prevent Coastal Travel Company from displaying or licensing the images to any third party. Available for individual images, a selection, or the entire delivery. Most commonly requested for flagship campaign launches.

03

Expanded Print Rights

Covers national and international print advertising — magazine spreads, newspaper inserts, billboard campaigns, and out-of-home advertising. Available as a one-time upgrade or bundled into The Signature Story from the outset.

On Delivery

Your License Certificate

Every project delivery includes a license certificate — a permanent record of the rights granted, delivered alongside your gallery access instructions.

The certificate records: your name and property, the collection and delivery date, the license scope (permitted uses, duration, geography), and the expiry date. It serves as documentation for any internal or third-party compliance requirements.

Your certificate is also accessible at any time through your client portal.
